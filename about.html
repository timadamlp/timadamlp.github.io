About Us

Learn about the story and mission behind Logo & Brand Design Hub

Our Mission

Logo & Brand Design Hub was established in 2023 with a mission to help startups and small businesses enhance their market competitiveness through professional brand identities. We believe that every business should have access to high-quality brand identity, regardless of size or budget.

In today's increasingly competitive market, a professional brand image is often the key differentiator between success and failure. However, many small businesses and entrepreneurs face barriers to accessing professional design services, including high costs and lack of expertise.

Our mission is to help small businesses overcome these barriers by providing unbiased tool reviews, practical design resources, and professional brand advice, enabling them to build strong brand identities in a cost-effective way.

Our Team

Our team consists of brand design experts and digital marketing consultants with over 10 years of experience, who deeply understand the importance of strong brand identity for business growth. Each team member is committed to providing genuine, practical advice to help clients make informed decisions.

Our Methodology

We rigorously test every design tool we recommend, evaluating its usability, design quality, price value, and customer support. Only tools that truly create value for small businesses receive our recommendation.

1. Comprehensive Testing

Our team personally uses each tool, from registration to final product export throughout the entire process, ensuring a thorough understanding of the user experience.

2. Multi-dimensional Evaluation

We evaluate tools from multiple angles, including usability, design quality, feature diversity, price value, and customer support.

3. Regular Updates

Design tools and platforms are constantly evolving. We regularly reassess and update our reviews to ensure information accuracy and timeliness.

4. User Feedback

We collect and integrate user feedback to provide comprehensive tool evaluations, including pros and cons across different scenarios.
